Manav's Popularity App

Welcome to my app! This super handy tool helps you see how popular someone is. More importantly, it allows you to easily visualize three dimensial data to figure out whether you're actually popular.
After extensive research, we identified three variables with the highest correlation to popularity:
  1. Number of Facebook friends (x-axis)
  2. Daily time (minutes) spent ignoring people (y-axis)
  3. Average size of lunch table cohort (radius)
